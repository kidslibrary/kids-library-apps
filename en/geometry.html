Kids' Library: Mathematics

Cards of shapes, bodies, colors and digits for early childhood development.




In application "Mathematics" from Kid's Library:
- the application is translated in English, Russian and Romanian
- 5 categories of objects, shapes, bodies, colors and digits
- 55 cards, voiced by a professional speaker
- slide show mode

​

Cards of shapes, bodies, colors and digits for early childhood development. The cards' title is professionally voiced in the studio.

Images for children can be studied by one of several ways. Each way is effective in its own manner and is based on Glenn Doman's well-known method of developing cards. The first method allows you to manually flip cards, while each picture being voiced. It's also possible to repeat the pronunciation by pressing on the image. The second method is suitable for the smallest ones, due to the fact that developing cards are shown as a slide show, so you can just watch the colorful images, listen to the pronunciation of the words and their writing.

Cards from each category can be flipped. Each card, when pressed, is voiced by the professional speaker.

In order to enjoy the application you need to remember a few simple steps:
1. Press once on the picture to listen to the name of the card
2. Swipe left or right to scroll through cards.
3. The button in the upper right corner serves to start or stop the slide show.
4. If you want to change the settings, select "Settings" in the main menu.

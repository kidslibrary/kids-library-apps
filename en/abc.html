Kids' Library: ABC

Cards with the letters of the alphabet for early childhood development.




In application "ABC" from Kids' Library:
- alphabets in several languages: English, Russian and Romanian
- cards with letters and images, voiced by a professional speaker
- true object's sounds in each card
- slide show mode

​

Cards with letters of the alphabet and images for early childhood development. All cards contain title and images. The title is professionally voiced in the studio.

Images for children can be studied by one of several ways. Each way is effective in its own manner and is based on Glenn Doman's well-known method of developing cards. The first method allows you to manually flip cards, while each picture being voiced. It's also possible to repeat the pronunciation by pressing on the image. The second method is suitable for the smallest ones, due to the fact that developing cards are shown as a slide show, so you can just watch the colorful images, listen to the pronunciation of the words and their writing.

Cards from each category can be flipped. Each card, when pressed, is voiced by the professional speaker. The possibility to play object sound is available for all the cards by a long press on or stroking the picture.

In order to enjoy the application you need to remember a few simple steps:
1. Press once on the letter to listen to its pronunciation
2. Press once on the picture to listen to the name of the card
3. The card's sound will be played by a long press or stroking the image
4. Swipe left or right to scroll through cards
5. The button in the upper right corner serves to start or stop the slide show
6. If you want to change the app settings, select the app from "Settings".
